# ROOT PROJECT CONTEXT

## Project Name

Sri Rama Optical

---

## Project Type

Real-world client project.

Modern optical showroom website developed for a running optical business.

This project is being developed as part of a B.Tech internship initiative involving real businesses and real clients.

---

## Business Overview

Sri Rama Optical is an established optical showroom with more than 45 years of experience.

The store provides:

- Optical frames
- Full frames
- Half frames
- Frameless glasses
- Transparent frames
- Kids frames
- Sunglasses
- Lens replacement
- Frame repairs
- Computerized eye testing
- Optical consultation

The business serves local customers and also supports selected domestic and international deliveries after direct confirmation.

---

## Project Goal

Create a professional digital showroom that:

- Builds trust immediately
- Highlights premium products
- Increases customer inquiries
- Encourages store visits
- Improves business visibility
- Provides a premium online presence

This is NOT an e-commerce website.

This is a digital showroom and customer inquiry platform.

---

## Target Customers

Primary Customers:

- Students
- Families
- Working professionals
- Senior citizens

Secondary Customers:

- Customers looking for branded frames
- Customers looking for customized lenses
- Customers seeking eye testing services

---

## Website Purpose

The website should allow customers to:

- Explore products
- Explore categories
- View available brands
- Learn about services
- Learn about eye testing
- Contact the store
- Call directly from mobile devices
- Navigate directly using Google Maps

The website should NOT focus on online ordering.

---

## Core Business Rules

### Rule 1

No online checkout.

### Rule 2

No shopping cart.

### Rule 3

No online payment gateway.

### Rule 4

No appointment booking system.

### Rule 5

Customer communication should happen through direct phone calls.

### Rule 6

All orders and delivery confirmations happen through direct contact with the store.

### Rule 7

Product information should prioritize visuals and trust over aggressive pricing.

---

## Trust Factors

The following trust points should be highlighted throughout the website:

- 45+ Years Experience
- Computerized Eye Testing
- Trusted Local Business
- Student Offers
- Fast Lens Preparation
- Lens Replacement Services
- Frame Repair Services
- Premium Brands
- Customer Reviews

---

## Design Philosophy

The website should feel:

- Premium
- Modern
- Trustworthy
- Clean
- Professional
- Family Friendly

The website should NOT feel:

- Cheap
- Overcrowded
- Flashy
- Distracting

---

## Mobile First Strategy

Most visitors are expected to use mobile devices.

Design priority:

1. Mobile
2. Tablet
3. Desktop

All layouts must be responsive.
