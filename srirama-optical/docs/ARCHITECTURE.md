# WEBSITE ARCHITECTURE

## Architecture Philosophy

Sri Rama Opticals is not an e-commerce platform.

The website acts as:

- Digital Showroom
- Business Identity
- Product Showcase
- Trust Building Platform
- Customer Inquiry Platform

Primary Goal:

Guide visitors from curiosity to direct contact.
