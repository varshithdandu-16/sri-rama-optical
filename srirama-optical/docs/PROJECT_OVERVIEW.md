# PROJECT OVERVIEW

## Project Name

Sri Rama Opticals Website

---

## Project Type

Real-World Client Project

---

## Project Description

Sri Rama Opticals is a trusted optical showroom with more than 45 years of experience.

This project aims to create a modern, premium, mobile-first digital showroom that helps customers:

- Explore optical products
- Discover available services
- Learn about computerized eye testing
- View featured collections
- Contact the store directly
- Navigate to the store location

---

## Business Goal

Build trust and increase customer inquiries through a professional online presence.

## Unique Selling Points

The website differentiates itself through:

- 45+ Years of Business Experience
- Computerized Eye Testing
- Premium Frame Showcase
- Direct Customer Communication
- Mobile-First Experience
- Professional Optical Consultation
- Trusted Local Business Reputation

---

## Key Features

- Premium Homepage
- Product Showcase
- Product Details Pages
- Category Browsing
- Eye Testing Information
- Student Offers
- International Delivery Information
- Customer Reviews
- Direct Calling
- Google Maps Integration

## Primary Conversion Goal

Primary Goal:

Generate direct customer phone calls.

Secondary Goals:

- Increase store visits
- Increase customer trust
- Showcase products professionally
- Improve brand visibility

Every major page should encourage users to contact the store.
