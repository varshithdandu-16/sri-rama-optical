# CONTENT STRATEGY

## Content Goal

The website content should build trust, encourage customer contact, and showcase products professionally.

---

## Tone of Voice

The website should sound:

- Professional
- Friendly
- Trustworthy
- Helpful
- Confident

---

## Avoid

Do not use:

- Fake urgency
- Misleading claims
- Overhyped marketing
- Excessive sales language

---

## Preferred Messaging

Good Examples:

- 45+ Years of Trusted Eye Care
- Computerized Eye Testing Available
- Premium Frames for Every Style
- Professional Optical Consultation

Avoid Examples:

- Best Optical Shop in the World
- Limited Time Only
- Buy Now Before It's Gone

---

## CTA Messaging

Primary CTA:

Call Now

Secondary CTA:

Explore Collection

Tertiary CTA:

Get Directions

---

## Customer Experience Goal

Every visitor should feel:

"Experienced professionals I can trust with my vision."

---

## Brand Personality

50% Trust

30% Premium

20% Family Friendly
